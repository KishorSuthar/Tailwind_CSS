The span element is inline by default, so padding on the left, right, and bottom will work, but padding on the top won’t be visible. Similarly, margin on the left and right will work, but margin on the top and bottom won’t have any effect. To make all padding and margin properties work as expected—while still keeping the element inline—you can add the inline-block class. This makes the span behave like a block element in terms of spacing but keeps it inline with other elements.
